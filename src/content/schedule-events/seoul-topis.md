In the afternoon of May 21st, 2026, our group visited **Seoul TOPIS** (Seoul Transport Operation and Information Service), the control centre that monitors and manages transport across the entire metropolis from the 5th floor of City Hall.

After regrouping from the morning's free exploration, the team gathered for a guided introduction to the centre. We were shown how TOPIS draws together live data from buses, the metro, taxis and roads onto a vast wall of screens, and how operators turn that flood of information into clear, real-time guidance for the public.

## What we learned

- How Seoul collects and integrates transport data from across the whole city.
- How that big data is processed and visualised for both operators and citizens.
- How a smart-city control centre detects incidents and keeps traffic flowing.

For computer-science students, TOPIS was a vivid, working example of data engineering and AI applied at the scale of a megacity, connecting the research we had seen all week to a system that millions of people rely on every day.

The group was dismissed directly after the visit.
