During our Seoul Study Tour, our group had the privilege of visiting Sungkyunkwan University (SKKU), one of Korea's most prestigious universities and a global leader in education and research. Founded originally in 1398 with a deep historical heritage, SKKU has a rich history of academic excellence seamlessly blended with modern innovation. We were warmly welcomed by faculty members and graduate researchers from the College of Computing and Informatics, who generously hosted us for a tour of their advanced computing labs and research facilities, showcasing their strong industry-aligned tech ecosystem.

![](/images/visit/skku/02.jpeg)

After the initial welcome, our hosts began by providing an overview of SKKU's computing and advanced software programs, highlighting the university's interdisciplinary approach that blends artificial intelligence, data science, software engineering, and smart-industry applications. They emphasized SKKU's exceptionally strong enterprise partnerships, particularly its deep collaboration with industry leaders like Samsung, and its mission to nurture world-class tech talent capable of tackling complex, real-world challenges. As we toured their computing facilities, the researchers introduced us to several ongoing projects, ranging from next-generation software platforms to intelligent systems, giving us a vivid picture of cutting-edge tech research in action.

![](/images/visit/skku/03.jpeg)

Overall, our visit to Sungkyunkwan University was an inspiring and enlightening experience. We gained valuable insights into the university's cutting-edge research and its strong commitment to fostering innovation and industry collaboration. The warm hospitality of our hosts and the opportunity to engage with leading researchers left a lasting impression, reinforcing the importance of global academic partnerships in advancing technology and education.
